Located in Thailand, Rayong is a place of culture and nature. A place filled with shrines and parks, you will feel the spirit of the land envelop you. Places as old as the Wat Pa Pradu to the more modern places such as the Rayong Dam, sparks an interest in everyone. Beaches fill the coast with beauty. Rayong is a great place for the entire family to enjoy with a vast amount of sights and choices.
