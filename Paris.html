    From the Notre Dame Cathedral to the Eiffel Tower, Paris provides a surplus of fun and exciting places you can visit. While in Paris, you can gaze up at the stunning Eiffel Tower, which stands an amazing 986 ft (301 m) tall! Take a trip to the enormous Notre Dame Cathedral that has been open since 1345! Walk across the Pont Neuf (New Bridge), which despites its name, is one of the oldest bridges in Paris! This bridge was the first road to separate the pedestrians and the traffic; also, it was the first bridge that did not allow horse passage. Have you ever seen a weather station that once was a church? Tour Saint Jacques, built in the 16th-century, is the former bell tower for the church of Saint-Jacques-de-la-Boucherie. After the church was dismantled in 1802, the belfry was chosen to be transformed into a weather station.
