Basic Info

  • Location: Craiova, Romania
  • Current Time Zone: Eastern European Time +2:00 hours
  • Currency Exchange: 0.28 USD = 1 Romanian Leu

Located in one of the five major cities in Romania, this park suppresses it all, leaving a tranquil visit. Began in 1901, created by 3 people, spent 2 years planting 50,000 seedlings of trees and shrubs; many of them not adapted to the environment. It began to take shape with its lake and islands, the bridges and the waterfalls, to create one of the most beautiful parks in the world. This is the second largest parks in Europe, and contains the biggest racecourse in the Balkan area. It was finally fenced off in 1956 but you are free to enter and explore.
