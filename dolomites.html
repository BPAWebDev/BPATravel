    Nothing says calm and serene like the great outdoor! The Dolomites, located in North Eastern Italy, is a vast mountain range that will be sure to bring out the adventurer in you! The mountains provide a great area biking, hiking, and horse riding for you and the family. The Belluno Dolomite National Park, which is located on the southernmost edge of the Dolomites, is special because it not only joins several mountain clusters together that once were preserved by separate reserves, but it spans 79,073 acres. Two of the most popular and beautiful mountain ranges would include Mount Serva and Vette Feltrine. The park was not founded until 1990; however, it has been visited over the last thousand years showing evidence of early human habitation. What else can you see at the wonderful Dolomites? There over 1,500 types of vascular vegetation and a wide range of wildlife, which would include: hedgehogs, weasels and Alpine hares.
