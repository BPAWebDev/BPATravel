Nothing says calm and serene like the great outdoors, and The Dolomites in North Eastern Italy provides just that. This vast land of this mountain range will bring out the adventurous nature in you. This land mass formed over 2000 million years ago in the primordial sea and is over 3,000 meters high. It is locked between 5 valleys: Isarco, Pusteria, Piave, Brent, Adige. The mountains provide a great area biking, hiking, and horse riding for you and the family.
